Equipment Maintenance Guide

1. Regular Inspections
   - Check temperature sensors daily
   - Monitor vibration levels weekly
   - Inspect electrical connections monthly

2. Failure Prevention
   - Replace filters every 3 months
   - Lubricate moving parts monthly
   - Calibrate sensors quarterly

   - Contact maintenance team for vibration > 0.5

4. Troubleshooting

   - Excessive vibration: Inspect bearings
   - Electrical issues: Verify connections and power supply
